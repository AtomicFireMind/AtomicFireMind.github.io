引言

作者开门见山，指出：在线性尺度空间对曲线结构进行几何建模及对其行为进行系统分析，可以达到很高的亚像素精度与准确性，实现无偏差提取。（Geometric modeling of curvilinear structures and systematic analysis of their behavior in linear scale-space leads to unbiased extraction results of very high sub-pixel precision and accuracy.）
